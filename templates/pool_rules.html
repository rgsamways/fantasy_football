Pick Seven — How to Play

  1. Each week, pick seven slots: QB, RB, WR, TE, K (individual players) + an Offensive Team (OFF) and a Defensive Team (DEF).
  2. Your score = player fantasy points (standard scoring) + (OFF pts scored − points DEF allowed).
  3. Each player and each team can only be used once per season in their respective slot — choose wisely.
  4. The player with the most total points at the end of the regular season wins.
  5. Picks lock once the admin processes results for that week.
  6. You may update your picks at any time before they lock.
  7. Other members' picks are hidden until the week is processed.

Scoring Formula

  • QB / RB / WR / TE: Pass TD +6, Pass Yd +0.1, Pass Att +0.2, Completion +0.5, INT −2  |  Rush TD +6, Rush Yd +0.1, Rush Att +0.2  |  Rec TD +6, Rec Yd +0.1, Reception +1  |  Fumble lost −3.
  • K: Kicking stats are not tracked — the K slot contributes 0 points.
  • OFF team: Points scored by that team in their game that week.
  • DEF team: Points that team allowed (opponent's score) — subtracted from your total.
  • Team component = OFF pts scored − DEF pts allowed. Pick a high-scoring offense and a stingy defense.

Weekly Winner & Tiebreakers

  • The player with the highest weekly total is highlighted in the standings.
  • Season tiebreaker: most weekly wins, then shared pool.
{% elif pool.pool_type == "PickEm" %}

PickEm — How to Play

  1. Each week, pick one NFL team.
  2. You earn the points that team scores in their game that week.
  3. You can only use each team once per season — choose wisely.
  4. The player with the most total points at the end of the regular season wins.
  5. Picks lock once the admin processes results for that week.
  6. You may change your pick for the current week at any time before it locks.
  7. Other members' picks are hidden until the week is processed.

Weekly Winner

  • The player who earns the most points in a single week is highlighted in the standings.
  • If multiple players tie for the week's high score, all are credited.

Tiebreakers

  • If two or more players finish with identical season totals, the player with more weekly wins takes precedence.
  • If still tied, the pool is shared.
{% else %}

Survivor — How to Play

  1. Each week, pick one NFL team to win their game.
  2. If your team wins, you advance to the next week. If they lose or tie, you're eliminated.
  3. You can only use each team once per season — choose wisely.
  4. The last player (or players) still alive at the end of the season wins.
  5. Picks lock once the admin processes results for that week.
  6. You may change your pick for the current week at any time before it locks.

Tiebreakers

  • If multiple players survive the entire season, the pool is shared.
  • If all remaining players are eliminated in the same week, those players share the pool.

Ties in NFL Games

  • An NFL game that ends in a tie counts as a loss for whoever picked either team.
