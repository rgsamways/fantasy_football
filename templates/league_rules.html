{{ league.league_type }} Specific Rules

{% if league.league_type == 'SuperFlex' %}

One offensive flex spot can be filled by any offensive position, including Quarterback (QB).

{% elif league.league_type == 'Keeper' %}

Teams can retain up to 3 players from their previous season's roster in exchange for draft picks.

{% elif league.league_type == 'Dynasty' %}

Teams retain their entire roster from year to year. Annual drafts consist only of rookies.

{% elif league.league_type == 'Roster Eliminator' %}

Each week, the lowest-scoring team must cut one player from their roster who then becomes a free agent.

{% elif league.league_type == 'Best Ball' %}

No lineup management is required. The system automatically calculates your optimal score from your entire roster each week.

{% else %}

Standard fantasy football scoring and roster rules apply.

{% endif %}

Salary Models

1. Points-Based

Salaries are directly tied to performance. Total Points * Multiplier (e.g. $1,000 per point).

2. Touchdown-Based

High stakes model. Total Touchdowns * $100,000. Kickers are capped at $100,000 maximum.

3. Performance Floor

Base minimum salary plus a bonus for every point scored above the league average.

4. Auction Value

Salary is the winning bid from the draft, with a fixed annual inflation for retained players.

5. NFL Cap Mirror

General Manager realism. Salaries are scaled mirrors of the player's actual NFL contract cap hit.

6. Tiered Positional

Fixed prices based on positional rank (e.g. Top 5 QBs all share the same elite price tag).
