Configurable Formats

When creating any of the league types above, you can further customize the experience with these settings:

📊 Scoring Formats

  • Standard: Traditional scoring where receptions do not grant extra points. Favors high-yardage and touchdown-dependent players.
  • PPR (Point Per Reception): Grants a full 1.0 point for every catch. significantly increases the value of wide receivers and pass-catching running backs.
  • Half-PPR: A balanced approach granting 0.5 points per reception. Currently the most popular format in modern fantasy football.

🏈 Roster Formats

  • Standard: The classic lineup: 1 QB, 2 RB, 2 WR, 1 TE, 1 Flex (RB/WR/TE), 1 K, and 1 DST.
  • SuperFlex: Adds a second Flex spot where a Quarterback can be started. This dramatically increases the value and scarcity of the QB position.

⚔️ Play Formats

  • Head-2-Head: Face off against a different league member every week. Your goal is simply to outscore your opponent for that week.
  • Season-long: No weekly matchups. Total points are tracked across the entire season, and the highest aggregate score wins it all.

New formats and customization options are added regularly! Check back soon for more ways to compete.
